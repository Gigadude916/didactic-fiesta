ANDREW KOSCHESKI

Andrew Koscheski is an art student at SJSU who specializes in photography. He has been taking classes in art for several years. Now he is working on art ideas and concepts that are not necessarily photographic. Some of his work has become new media, concept, or design based this semester. He will continue to make unique works and exploring new ideas and ways to make art.
